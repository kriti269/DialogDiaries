Privacy Policy

Dialog Diaries is governed by following privacy policy.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. Our team constantly keeps working on what data is exposed to users and find any loopholes to prevent misuse of information posted here.

The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to inform you about what and how much information is collected by us and how it may be used. This policy only applies to this blogging website.

What you share voluntarily:

We may collect personal information from you such as name and email address to use that in sending newsletters, a future enhancement to this website, which you can unsubscribe from anytime you want. In addition, when you create an account with us, we store your username, however the password is encrypted and is not visible to us.

Automatically collected information:

Currently, we do not track ip addresses or any device information from which the website was accessed. However, to enhance security of the blog, this part of information will be accessed in the near future.

Cookies:

Cookies and session storage are used for the sole purpose of maintaining sessions when you are logged in. There are some persistent cookies to store data whenever you exit your browser to keep you logged in until your session expires or deleted manually.

Third-party information sharing:

We absolutely do not share information with any third party apps or vendors.

Contact when in doubt!

Should you have any queries or information to share regarding our privacy policies or its misuse, you can always reach out to us at: Contact Us
